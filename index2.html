ABOUT US

Started In 2003, Enovators was firstly named Resplendence a part of Vishwa Bharati Public School's
series of Inter-School Events. This club had a first team of 3 members. With time its popularity increased and was hence named e-novators in the year 2005. Soon we started our own inter-school
competitions that included various events. It was renamed Enovators.
