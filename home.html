What are E-Resources ?

An Electronic Resource is defined as a resource
which requires computer and internet access.
It can be any type of product or data that delivers
information to people like text, images, videos etc.

?

What is E-Learning ?

E-Learning refers to the use of Internet
Technology to enhance knowledge,skills
and know-how. E-Learning can be an init-
iated programme in an institution, corpora-
tion and at an individual level. It is a new
and a very good approach to learning.
